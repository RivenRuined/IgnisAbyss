Ignis x Abyss – Life x Death

This build features three attacks: Burst (Space, gold effect), Auto Nova Pulse (every ~3s, killing up to 5 tendrils), and SuperNova (requires 10s fill; triggered by button or V key, kills all tendrils).
Movement is controlled by WASD/Arrow keys or touchscreen – by a single “Movement” slider that’s modified by health.
